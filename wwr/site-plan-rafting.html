Overview

Purpose

We are a growing water rafting company that desires to capture the attention of the current generation to dive into the sport and recreational activity of water rafting. And it is evidently that among places that could put our word out is a well crafted informative website for our clients to get better insights about us, the sport and what to expect. We hope from this we shall be able to generate more leads and increase our revenues

Audience

The target is individuals with age range 18-50years both male and female adventerous in nature with middle to high income who are looking for that next adventure with some addrenaline rush. It is expected the audience to access our website from both mobile and laptop/desktop computer.
